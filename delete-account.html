Delete Your FlexMeal Account

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

FlexMeal, operated by Ngo, respects your right to delete your account and associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request, what data will be permanently removed, and what data must be retained for legal and financial compliance.

How to Delete Your Account

FlexMeal offers two ways to delete your account:

Option 1 — In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest method and takes effect immediately:

  1. Open the FlexMeal app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Option 2 — Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to netngo23@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account.
  3. Include the email address associated with your FlexMeal account in the message body.
  4. We will process your request within 30 days.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following personal data is permanently removed:

  • Account profile (name, email address, phone number, avatar)
  • Order history
  • In-app messages with chefs and customers
  • Push notification tokens
  • Saved preferences (cart, location, language)
  • Uploaded photos (meal photos, profile photos)
  • Chef account information (if applicable)

What Data Is Retained

Some data must be retained to comply with legal and financial obligations:

  • Financial transaction records — Retained for 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations. This data is not linked to your active account and is not used for any other purpose.
  • Anonymized analytics data — Aggregate usage data that has been fully anonymized and is no longer tied to your identity, used only for service improvement.
  • Legal compliance records — Only if retention is required by applicable law.

Timeline

  • In-app deletion: Immediate upon confirmation.
  • Email request deletion: Processed within 30 days of your request.
  • Backup purge: Backups are purged within an additional 30 days following deletion.
